About Deck

Deck is an amazing place to make your very own address on the web. These are called "Cards", and they display your information. Your name, bio, and a button linking to your other things. This could be a Kickstarter Campaign, personal website, anything!

Deck gets updated frequently, and is always improving. The current version is 1.0.0.
